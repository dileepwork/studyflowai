# StudyFlow AI - Intelligent Syllabus Analyzer

This application uses Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Graph Theory to analyze your syllabus and generate a personalized study plan.

## Features
- **PDF/TXT Upload**: Extract content from your syllabus files.
- **Dependency Detection**: Automatically identify which topics depend on others.
- **Topological Sorting**: Get the most logical order to study your topics.
- **Difficulty Classification**: Topics are categorized as Easy, Medium, or Hard.
- **Smart Scheduling**: Generates a weekly plan based on your available time.

## How to use
1. Upload your syllabus (PDF or TXT).
2. Configure your study duration and hours.
3. Click "Generate Study Flow".
4. View your Study Roadmap and Weekly Schedule!

## Technology Stack
- **Backend**: Python, Flask, SpaCy, NetworkX, Scikit-learn
- **Frontend**: React, Vite, Framer Motion, Recharts, Lucide Icons
